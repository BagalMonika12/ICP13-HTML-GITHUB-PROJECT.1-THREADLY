Threadly is a modern clothing platform created to bring trendy, affordable, and comfortable fashion to everyone. Whether you're dressing for a casual day out or a special occasion, Threadly has something for you.

Our goal is to make online shopping simple and accessible — even in a pure HTML format! This project was built with passion, creativity, and teamwork to showcase a basic e-commerce clothing website using only HTML.

  • Womens Fashion :- Dresses, Tops, Kurtas
